About GYS Syndicate

GYS Syndicate is more than just a hand cricket group — it’s a community, a culture, and a celebration of strategy, reflex, and friendship. Born out of pure passion and backyard competition, GYS Syndicate has evolved into a structured, rule-based hand cricket league that brings out the true spirit of the game.

Our Mission

To make hand cricket recognized as a thrilling and competitive format, while creating lifelong memories and friendships. We aim to maintain fair play, intense rivalry, and pure entertainment — both on and off the field.

What We Offer

  • Organized hand cricket tournaments and leagues
  • Player auctions and team management systems
  • Live scoring and match tracking apps
  • Stats, leaderboards, and awards
  • A strong and passionate player community

Why GYS Stands Out

  • Unique rules for a professional hand cricket format
  • 11-player teams with full innings and bowling stats
  • Creative events, challenges, and rivalries
  • Focus on discipline, respect, and sportsmanship
