❓ Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is this website for?
It offers interactive quizzes, cricket challenges, and educational tools for curious minds and sports lovers.

Yes, all quizzes on this platform are completely free to use and enjoy.
3. Can I suggest a quiz topic?
Definitely! Use the contact form or email us your suggestion anytime.
4. Will you add more cricket content?
Yes! We regularly update with fresh trivia, records, and expert-level cricket puzzles.
5. Do I need to create an account?
No registration is required — just open and play!
6. Can I track my score?
Basic quizzes don't store results, but we’re working on leaderboard and user profiles soon.

Yes, some quizzes include countdown timers for added challenge.

Absolutely. The site is mobile-optimized for all screen sizes.

Yes! After finishing a quiz, you can screenshot or copy your score to share it.
10. Will there be a leaderboard?
We're working on global and local leaderboards to foster competitive fun.
11. Do you have IPL or Test cricket quizzes?
Yes! We cover all formats — IPL, Tests, ODIs, and T20s, including history and stats.
12. Can I request team-based trivia?
Sure! We love building content around user requests like team challenges or era-based quizzes.
13. Do you cover women's cricket too?
Yes. We’re expanding quizzes on women's cricket legends, records, and matches.

You're already in dark mode! We design for comfort and style.
